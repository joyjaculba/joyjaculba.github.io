About

Welcome to Joy Marie’s Web Development World! I am a passionate and dedicated web developer with a flair for crafting digital experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Introduction

My coding journey began when I was studying my Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology. While studying, I was able to participate and win the Provincial Technical Education And Skills Development Authority Skills Competition in Web Developing, and advanced to Regional level hailing as the Champion representing our province. Through then I gained a solid foundation in coding principles and discovered my love for turning ideas into interactive digital experiences.

Venturing into the industry, I had the privilege of working with a small but brilliant team. With this team, I was able to expand my skill set. From collaborating on intricate web applications to contributing to large-scale-e-commerce platforms.

I had experiences in a variety of technologies, including the LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP), Magento, Shopify, WordPress, and Laravel. This eclectic skill set has allowed me to tackle a wide range of projects, each presenting unique challenges and opportunities for growth.
